The Evening Unfolds

How It Works

No acting experience required. Just a willingness to play along.

01 ✉️
Receive Your Invitation

Your host sends you a link with a party code. Create your account, answer a few quick questions about how you'd like to play, and choose your character.

02 🎭
Become Someone Else

You'll receive a full character dossier — your background, your secrets, and your relationship to every other guest. Study it well.

03 🍷
Arrive & Mingle

Cocktail hour begins the intrigue. Gather clues, forge alliances, trade secrets, and decide who you trust — and who you don't.

04 🔍
Solve the Mystery

Over dinner, evidence surfaces, revelations are made, and the evening builds to a final accusation. Only one person knows the truth. Will you uncover them?
