The Experience

An Evening Unlike
Any Other

Part dinner party, part escape room, part theatre. Every guest plays a character. Every clue matters. And someone at your table has blood on their hands.

01
You're Invited
Guests receive their character assignment, backstory, and the evening's details via our app. The mystery begins before you arrive.
02
Cocktail Hour
Arrive in character. Mingle, gather intel, complete social missions. The host is very much alive — for now. Secrets are already circling the room.
03
The Murder
The host is found dead. The investigation opens. Evidence surfaces through documents, QR codes, locked boxes, and your fellow guests' secrets.
04
The Reveal
Three acts of escalating tension. The killer plays their role until the final accusation. When the truth comes out — it's unforgettable.
